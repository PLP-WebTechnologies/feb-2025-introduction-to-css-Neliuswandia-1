Welcome to My Pet's page

This is Rusty. He is a golden retriever, a furry beacon of happiness. His tail, a constant metronome,
thumps against furniture in joyous rhythms. His large, soulful eyes radiate unwavering affection, and his
playful barks echo through the house. He adores chasing tennis balls, digging in the garden, and, most
importantly, receiving endless belly rubs.
